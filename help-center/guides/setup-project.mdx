## Overview

Use this guide as a sample for your real setup instructions.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new file" icon="file-plus">
    Add `my-page.mdx` with a title and short description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write the first section" icon="edit">
    Add a simple header and a few sentences users can scan quickly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add it to navigation" icon="list">
    Update `documentation.json` so the page appears in the sidebar.
  </Step>
</Steps>
